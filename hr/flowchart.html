Flow Chart For New Team Member

STEP ONE

Interviewing the candidate. If the candidate qualified for selection please send the candidate a onboarding email . Please ensure that the candidate is not employed at any other organisation or has different profession. In that case you will need to complete a conflict of Interest Form . If the candidate is selected for the position please provide a copy of Fair Work Information Statement

STEP TWO

Once the candidate completes the training and has uploaded all the documents, an email will be sent to hr@decentcare.com.au.
Please Follow the steps below

  1. Verify all the documents to ensure that there are no expired documentation
  2. If the candidate is on an Australian Visa, check the working conditions of the visa.
  3. Place all the documents in the DC Cloud under the new team members name
  4. After the verification of the Documents draft a contract that can be sent vid Panda Doc along with other documents

STEP THREE

In this step an account needs to be created in the LookOut App
Please Follow the steps below

  1. After the verification a profile in LookOut needs to be created. LookOut Dashboard
  2. Create a Helper under the Helper section
  3. Upload all the Documentation in the Helper Verification
  4. Upload the Contract in the verification with the contract due date.
  5. Approve the ticket, this would have been sent initially to hr@decentcare.com.au when the candidate uploaded all the documentation
  6. After the approval an email should be sent to accounts@decentcare.com.au to set up the payroll for the new team member and book an appointment for meet and greet session

STEP FOUR

After Commencing the work an appointment needs to be booked after a month to ensure all the OH&S protocol has been followed and the worker is being provided with appropriate breaks and is happy with the Team Leader or managers.
